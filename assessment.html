Professional Self Assessment:

I returned to college in 2023 with the goal of finally graduating with a degree in computer science. After taking a break to focus on my rapidly developing personal and family life, I decided it was finally time to get back on the saddle and get this done for myself. In my time at SNHU I was blessed with patient professors who were willing to work with me in any situation life pulled me into. I was taught and sculpted into what I am today. I've always been passionate about computers and the "how" of their many operations, and I credit this largelyto my allergy to the outside (unless its Bermuda Grass).

While enrolled in the Computer Science program at SNHU, I have learned invaluable skills and an innumerable amount of skills. I feel as though I have improved greatly in my attention to detail and my ability to spot small, simple mistakes that I missed when I first started my courses. The biggest example of this rests in this repository, as my simple grocer app didn't even fuction when it was submitted, and now here it sits as a fully functional inventory tracking app. I learned the values of staying organized in your work, and the value that testing brings to the table in important projects.

These courses taught me the importance of the development lifecycle, and as a result, I now plan out my every assignment or project to ensure I stay on track and deliver working code with each submission without ever feeling rushed or panicked about the quality I will deliver. I learned to take time to understand clients needs and work to make sure they are met and satisfied through the use of the SDLC. Through my time within this program, and throughout my capstone assignment, I was shown the importance of the following soft skills that I lacked when entering: Critical thinking, creative problem solving, attention to detail, and the value of collaboration and asking for help when it's needed.

I feel as though I also gained valuable hard skills such as web and mobile development, data analysis, programming, and software development. As I continue on my journey in the field of computer science, I am excited to apply the knowledge and skills I have gained to real-world projects and contribute to the development of innovative solutions. All of this and more has transformed me into a confident and capable software engineer who is hungry to learn more, and will not be satisfied unless I give it my all.
